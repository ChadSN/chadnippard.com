Blood Lust GGJ 2022

Play the game here: https://fydar.github.io/GlobalGameJam2022/

During the 2022 Global Game Jam in Brighton, I spent two intense days creating a variety of low-poly assets for a game called Blood Lust, themed around duality, where players take on the role of a vampire in a quaint village. My contributions included a diverse set of props, ranging from a sword and bucket to more elaborate structures like a well, a bridge, and even a church with gravestones. I also crafted trees and vegetation to bring the setting to life. The aesthetic of the game can be best described as "Gothic Rural," evoking the sombre and mysterious atmosphere of an old-world village with simple stonework, shadowy graveyards, and a distinctly vampiric charmâ€”perfect for the lurking vampire protagonist.
