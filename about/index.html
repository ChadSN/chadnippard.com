Hey there!
I'm Chad Nippard

I'm an undergraduate studying Computer Science for Games at the University of Brighton. Born and raised in Brighton, I'm passionate about writing, designing, and most of all, creating immersive worlds and games. Whether it's crafting digital experiences or bringing new ideas to life, I'm always excited to build and explore new possibilities.

My Career so far

Over the years, my hobbies in creative writing, digital design, and game development grew alongside various simple jobs, where I've worked on solo and collaborative game projects, continually honing my skills. I began freelancing as a logo designer, before shifting my career focus to data analysis. It was during this time that I made the decision to fully commit to the gaming industry. I applied to the University of Brighton for a degree in Computer Science for Games. Now, as a full-time student, I am focused on pursuing my passion, with aspirations to build a career in the gaming industry upon completing my degree.
