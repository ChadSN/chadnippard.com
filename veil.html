Veil: Alter Unknown

A demo is available from steam: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2470800

I designed this idea for Peter while working with him on Veil: Alter Unknown.

This linear 3D platforming challenge takes players through a dangerous lava tube, requiring precise movement to avoid the flowing lava below. Players use a grappling hook to swing between anchor points and glide to keep momentum, with platforms available for quick recovery if they fall. The level design tests agility and timing, creating a thrilling experience of skilfully navigating a hazardous environment while narrowly escaping the dangers of molten lava.
